'possibilities of voiceover' workshop with nicolás pereda at CEMA

Yesterday afternoon a group of artists met at the Center for Ethnographic Media Arts at USC for a day-long workshop with filmmaker Nicolás Pereda. Our focus was on the possibilities of voiceover, departing from studies of Agnès Varda to producing our own works of narration. We were to choose a photograph and make a video essay.

This photograph was taken in February 2023, at a performance choreographed at the Hudson River Park. The first performance of this piece occurred in 1971 by avant-garde artist Seung-taek Lee, on Nanji Island on the Han River in Seoul
