house of mu (2026) - This poem is based on the 1967 program House of Dust by Alison Knowles and James Tenney, and modified by Andrea Kim in 2026, based on code by Nick Monfort from 2014, 2024. This poem uses keywords generated from topic modeling the transcripts of 45 interviews of community leaders on Skid Row via the Walk the Talk archive (2014-2024), curated by the Los Angeles Poverty Department. From a selection of “stop words” from the corpus that the topic modeling algorithm excluded from its keyword generation, this poem inserts phrases from an in-depth interview conducted with Lorainne Morland about her lived experiences on Skid Row. The repetition of Morland’s words are opportunities to intervene on what the algorithm excludes and brings to question the matter of textual life.
