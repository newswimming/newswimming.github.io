GEN MU

GEN MU are experiments in virtual identity (meta-personae and the programmatic voice) that blend personal documentary, generative folklore, and computational fictions. These fictions are composed of spatial (XR) to temporal (AI) figurations grounded in performance and audiovisual media. A mix of performance art, film, and animation, the making of the House of Mu hypertext (work-in-progress) is ultimately both arts research and practice in theorizing alternative simulation logics rooted in cultural memory and reparative creative work.
